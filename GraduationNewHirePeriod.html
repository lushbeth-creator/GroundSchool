Graduation from New Hire Period

  1. 1
    All New Hire Training curriculum and expectations are completed.
  2. 2
    Attend Monthly Learning Lift-Off Webinars.
  3. 3
    New Hire Minimum Performance Expectation is achieved:
    • Hire Month (if mid-month): Pipeline Development
    • Full Month 1: 1+ Deal Installed ($2,000 in margin)
    • Full Month 2: 2+ Deals Installed ($4,000 in margin)
    • Full Month 3: 2+ Deals Installed ($6,000 in margin)
    • Full Month 4: 3+ Deals Installed ($8,000 in margin)
